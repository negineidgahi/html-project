This is were I write about my travel diaries! This time I am writing about my trip to Agra, India where I visisted the magnificent masterpiece, Taj Mahal. An immense mausoleum of white marble, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favourite wife. The Taj Mahal is the jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage.

Taj Mahal

Interesting facts about Taj Mahal:

  • Built by 22,000 laborers!
  • Shah Jahan intended to build another Taj Nahal in black marble across the river but a war with his sons did not make that possible.
  • The art and architecture took 17 years to complete.
  • Most famous feature is the white dome also known as the 'onion dome' with height of 35 meters.
  • Attracting more that a million tourists every year.
  • If it was built today it would cost US$100.
